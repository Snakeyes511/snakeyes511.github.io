Why use frame as our name?

We use frame here at Rent-A-Frame as a term to easily describe ourselves as a mech and exo suit renter and seller. It refers to the skeleton of a mech and exo suit and thus the use of frame rather than mech or exo suit.

Isn't it dangerous to rent/sell these frames to people

Nonsense, our state of the art screening process determines the motive and predicts the possible future use of our frames. We dont make trades with warmongers.
